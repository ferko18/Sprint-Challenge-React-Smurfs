1.  Explain the differences between `client-side routing` and `server-side routing`.
server-side routing: on every click on link,  a new page is requested from the server and the page refreshes. 
Routing is handeled by the server. 
client-side routing: The entire page is loaded initially , when a link is clicked , only data is updated. 
The page doen't refresh.Routing is handeled by javascript in the brouser.
